【专题】公共英语二_小作文

发表于2021-11-17|更新于2022-02-10|研究生入学考试
|字数总计:2.6k|阅读时长:11分钟|阅读量:

道歉信模板

表明写信目的：I'm writing this letter in purpose of expressing my apology to you for ___.

解释道歉原因：In retrospect, I believe the stupid mistake resulted form ___.

提出补救措施：To solve this problem, I intend to ____.

再次表示歉意：Please accept my most cordial and humblest apologies once again.

感谢信模板

表明写信目的：I'm writing this letter in purpose of extending my heartfelt gratitude to you for ___.

解释感谢原因：I have found ___ you have provided very informative and useful.

提出回报措施：My appreciation to you for your ___ is beyond words and I wish I could repay it one day.

再次表明谢意：Thank you again from the bottom of my heart for ___.

建议信模板

表明写信目的：I'm writing this letter in purpose of making some suggestions for ___.

介绍具体建议：It is imperative that ___.

客套话—望建议被采纳：I hope you will find these proposals useful, and I would be ready to discuss this matter with you to further details.

推荐信模板

表明写信目的：I'm writing this letter in purpose of recommending ___ to you.

介绍推荐理由：The first reason for my recommendation is . Besides/Moreover, .

客套话—望建议被采纳：Hope you enjoy ___. I am looking forward to discussing it with you later.

邀请信模板

表明写信目的：On behalf of __ , I'm honoured to write this letter to cordially invite you to attend __.

陈述邀请的内容：When & Where __ is going to be held in __ on __ , which will last from __ to __ .

介绍邀请理由：Considering that you are an outstanding expert in __, worshipped by all of us, we sincerely expect your attendance.

介绍被邀请访参加的活动环节：We cordially expect you to participate in ___.

补充说明其他相关信息：The schedule of the conference is attached for your reference.

再次邀请：We cordially hope that you would like to accept the invitation.

期待回复：I am looking forward to your reply, and waiting to meet you.

感谢信模板

感谢来信：I would likee to extend to you my heartfelt thanks for your ___ .

作出回应：I am extremely honored to accept your invitation to ___ .

陈述答复的具体内容：The following are some key points to pay particular attention to.

再次感谢来信：Thank you again from the bottom of my heart for your invitation.

期待答复：I am looking forward to hearing from you soon.

告示模板

表明告示意图：__ (数量) volunteers are urgently needed to coordinate the related matters of __ (活动名称).

介绍活动信息：__ (活动名称) is going to be held in __ (地点) on __ (日期), which will last from __ to __ .

介绍活动目的：The Students' Union is going to hold a __ , with the aim of __ .

介绍活动内容：Participants are greatly encouraged to take part in activities, such as __ .

阐述志愿者条件：Preference will be given to those __ .

提供联系方式：If you are interested in it, please send your resume, as soon as possible, to xxxx@outlook.com.

2008_Writing_PartA

中文思路：

开头段

  1. 我从加拿大回国后，当我打开行李时，发现了上个月问你借的音乐CD。
  2. 因此，我写这封信，想要向你道歉。

主体段

  1. 回想起来，我认为这个愚蠢的错误是由我的粗心造成的。
  2. 琐事使得我无暇打包行李。
  3. 如果我当时多注意，就不会忘记把你最喜欢的披头士音乐CD还给你了。

结尾段

  1. 为了解决这一问题，我打算用快递将CD寄还给你。
  2. 此外，作为我疏忽的补偿，我会把一些中国的特产一并寄给你。
  3. 我真心希望你可以接受我的道歉。

英文范文：

Dear Bob,

​ As I was unpacking the luggage after my return from Canda, I found the music CD borrowed from you there last month.(交代背景) So I'm writting this letter in purpose of expressing my apology to you.(表明写信目的)

​ In retrospect, I believe the stupid mistake resulted from my carelessness.(解释道歉原因) Trifles left me insufficient time to pack up. Had I paid more attention, I wouldn't forget to return your favourite CD of the Beatles to you.

​ To solve the problem, I intend to send this CD to you via Express Mail Service.(提出补救措施) Additionally, some specialities of China will be sent together as the compensation for my oversight. I really hope that you will be able to accept my apology.(再从表明歉意)

Yours Sincerely, Li Ming

2009_Writing_PartA

中文思路

开头段

  1. 正如您关于“白色污染”的报道中所说的那样，在一些地区，塑料袋使用规定并没有被有效地执行。
  2. 我写这封信，是为了表达本人对于塑料袋滥用的担忧，以及提供一些建议。

主体段

  1. 在我看来，限制塑料袋的使用，以避免对环境造成不可挽回的伤害，对我们而言是十分必要的。
  2. 为了解决这一难题，我想提出如下两个实用的建议。
  3. 首先，这些一次性的袋子应当以高价出售，这样一来人们就不会频繁使用了。
  4. 第二，政府应当鼓励使用绿色环保的替代品，比如纸袋或者布袋。

结尾段

我希望您觉得上述提议有益，并呼吁公众增强环保意识。

英文范文

Dear Deitor,

​ As your report on "White Pollution" indicates, regulations on the use of plastic bags have not been implemented effectively in some areas.(交代背景) I am writing this letter to express my concern over the abuse of plastic bags and make some suggestion.(表明写信目的)

​ In my opinion, it is imperative for us to limit the use of plastic bags in purpose of avoiding irretrievable damage on our environment. To crack this hard nut, I would like to propose two practical recommendations as follows. Firstly, these disposable bags should be sold at a high price so that people would not use them frequently. Secondly, governments should encourage the use of green and eco-friendly alternatives, such as paper or cloth bags.(介绍具体建议)

​ I hope you will find the proposals above conducive and call on the public to enhance the awareness of environmental protection.(客套话—望建议被采纳)

Yours Sincerely,

Li Ming

2013_Writing_PartA

中文思路

开头段

  1. 我谨代表学生会写这封信，诚挚地邀请您担任英语演讲比赛尊贵的评委。

主体段

  1. 此次比赛将于 3 月 15 日在我校大礼堂举办，进行的时间是 18:00 至 20:30。
  2. 参赛者首先需要在八分钟内进行关于“创新”主题的事先准备好的演讲，然后进行相关的问答环节。
  3. 考虑到您是一位杰出的英语语言专家，深受所有同学的尊敬，我们真诚地希望您对他们的表现进行打分和点评。
  4. 您的出席一定会让参赛者和现场观众都受益匪浅，更重要的是，也一定能够激发他们说英语的激情和信心。

结尾段

  1. 我们衷心地希望您能接受此次的邀请。
  2. 期待您的出席。

英文范文

Dear Mr.Reed,

​ On behalf of the Students' Union, (表明身份) I am writing this letter to earnestly invite you to be our honorable judge for our English speech contest.(表明写信目的)

​ The contest is to be held in our auditorium on March 15, which will last from 18:00 to 20:30. (介绍活动时间) Each contestant is required to first give a prepared speech on innovation within 8 minutes, and then a question-and-answer session follows. (介绍活动内容) Since you are an outstanding expert in English language venerated by all students, we sincerely expect you to score and make comments on their performance. Your attendance will surely benefit greatly both the competitors and the audience, more importantly, inspire their enthusiasm in speaking English with full confidence. (介绍邀请理由)

​ We cordially hope that you would like to accept the invitation. (再次邀请) We are looking forward to your attendance. (期待出席 / 回复)

Yours sincerely,

Li Ming

2016_Writing_PartA

中文思路

开头段

  1. 我写这封信是从心底感谢你的祝贺。
  2. 既然你问我关于翻译的建议，我想要跟你分享一些我自己的提高翻译的方法和经验

主体段

  1. 首先，翻译能力的提高是一个持续的过程，需要长期实践的积累。
  2. 具体来说，我强烈建议你要坚持不懈地加强包括听、读和写的英汉语基本技能。
  3. 更重要的是，要在大量的翻译实践中完善语法、逻辑和修辞方面的知识。
  4. 最后，想要成为一名优秀的译者，不仅要学习前人的翻译技巧，还要在此基础上形成自己的翻译体系。

结尾段

  1. 我希望以上建议对你有益。
  2. 我确信你会在不久的将来成为一名出色的译者。
  3. 期待着你的好消息。

英文范文

Dear Jack,

​ I'm writing this letter in purpose of thank you from the bottom of my heart for your congratulations.(表示感谢) Since you asked for advice on translation, I would like to share with you some of my own methods and experience as follows.(表明写信目的)

​ First of all, the improvement of translation ability is proving to be a continuous process involving an accumulation of long-term practice. Specifically speaking, you are highly suggested to make unremitting efforts to strengthen both English and Chinese basic skills, including listening, reading and writing. What's more, knowledge on grammar, logic and rhetoric should also be perfected through a great deal of practice. Finally, in order to be an outstanding translator, it is crucial to not only learn from previous translation techniques, but also form your own set of translation system based on them.(介绍具体建议)

​ I hope you will find these proposals useful, and I would be ready to discuss this matter with you to further details.(望意见被采纳) I'm sure you will become an excellent translator in the near future. I'm looking forward to hearing that news.(表达祝贺)

Yours sincerely,

Li Ming

2010_Writing_PartA

中文思路

第一段

  1. 我校将于下个月举行关于全球化的国际会议，现需要招募志愿者。
  2. 在会议期间，我们将组织各种各样的活动，比如讲座、研讨会和问答环节

第二段

  1. 志愿者的基本要求是精通英语、法语和日语。
  2. 我们将非常欢迎熟悉会议主题，有良好人际交往能力的志愿者。
  3. 在国际会议以及其他类似活动中有相关经验者将会优先考虑。
  4. 如果你对此志愿者活动感兴趣的话，请请尽快将您的简历发送至邮箱 volunteers@unn.com.cn，或者致电 7878778 咨询面试信息。
  5. 理想的人选将于下周由研究生会统一安排参加面试。

英文范文

Notice

December 15, 2010

​ Volunteers are needed for the international conference on globalization to be held next month on our campus. (表明告知意图) During the period, we will organize a variety of activities, such as lectures, seminars, and question-answer sessions. (介绍活动信息)

​ The basic requirement for volunteers is proficiency in English, French and Japanese. And those who are familiar with the theme of the conference and have good interpersonal skills will be highly welcomed. Preference will be given to those with relevant experience in international conferences or other similar activities. (阐明志愿者需具备的条件) If you are interested in it, please send your resume, as soon as possible, to volunteers@unn.com.cn , or call 7878778 for any information on interview. (提供联系方式) Ideal candidates will be arranged by Postgraduates' Association to attend interviews in the coming week.

Postgraduates' Association
